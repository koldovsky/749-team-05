Community Operations & Support Specialist


About the job

Cloudy Group’s core is a global community of expert software testers. Community team helps to onboard, support and nurture our testers providing them meaningful career and endless growth opportunities. Our ultimate vision is to give every tester an opportunity to develop their professional skills and enjoy building a software testing career feeling engaged and fulfilled.

Key responsibilities

- Providing professional help and support to our testers' community through monitoring and engaging in relevant discussions and Q&A in our community communication channels (e.g. Slack, Intercom, email).
- Contributing to the testers’ quality monitoring process and addressing any performance-related concerns to ensure high quality of delivered testing results.
- Providing regular guidance to community members, organizing processes for efficient two-way feedback.
- Mapping community know-how, creating knowledge base articles, sharing important updates and news with community members.
- Supporting our QA team with project-based tester recruitment and management.
- Working closely together with Community, Product, QA, Finance and other teams to support our testers' growth and development.
- Helping our Product Managers with gathering input for community-related product development, including writing user stories and requirements for new features.
- Continually evaluating Testlio's internal and external processes and workflows. Recommending possible improvements that will keep us moving in the right direction.

What do we expect from you?

- Excellent communicator with an ability to use both data and intuition to form decisions.
- Being tech-savvy and fascinated with technology.
- Being able to request, collect, and analyze data to make data-driven decisions.
- Experience in client-facing communication - support, project management or similar role.
- General understanding of software development lifecycle, preferably accompanied with a degree in computer science or similar.
- Thriving in a fast-paced and challenging environment, being able to learn and adapt quickly.
- Taking initiative, working independently and efficiently without close supervision.
- You have a desire to contribute to building the greatest global community of software testers.
- You love working with people and have a passion to change the world.
- You #lovetesting. Hands-on experience in different areas of software testing is a strong plus.

Software Engineer

About the job

Cloudy Group is growing fast. We need strong software engineers to meet the challenges of a growing startup. Our software engineers are responsible for building modern and fast single page web applications and REST APIs, using tools like ES6, React, Node.js, PHP, the Flux application architecture, SCSS and more. You will be a part of a product team that is focused on one of the areas of Cloudy Group roadmap and owning it end-to-end as much as possible to reduce dependencies. You will closely work with the product manager, designer, and other engineers to implement necessary features for the Cloudy Group platform. You will be able to dig into various parts of application development as well as give your voice in architecture-related decisions.

Key responsibilities

- Design and implement modern single page applications using Node.js, PHP, ES6, React and the Flux architecture.
- Partner with Product Manager and Design to solve problems - get things done and enable value proposition.
- Own, operate and maintain your team's services across all development environments, including production.
- Be proud of your work. Beside good code, you also value the impact and seek for validation via telemetry or direct customer feedback.
- Eager to learn continuously new things. You celebrate if you can learn new things, regardless of the matter (e.g. business, technology or some good anecdote).

What do we expect from you?

- You are a team player - individual goals come second, team goals come first.
- You have experience in building web applications.
- Proven track record of success and failures - we believe in experience and learning. Not everything goes right. You know your failures, learned from it and know how to avoid it.
- Knowledge and experience in JavaScript, Node.js, React, HTML, CSS.
- Experience with version control systems, preferably Git and Github.
- Experience working with REST and/or HTTP API's.
- Knowledge of relational databases and data modeling.
- Full English professional proficiency.

QA Project Manager

- Experience in leading a QA team.

Test Lead

About the job

Each of our clients is important and has signed up for the highest-quality testing available. We aim to deliver results that consistently exceed expectations. As our client base continues to grow at high speed, we’re looking for in-house Test Leads to join our QA team. Test Leads are essential to our success. They help determine project scope, manage test cycles, and report results. Test Leads work closely with our testers as well as our QA Project Managers to ensure testing meets Cloudy Group’s quality standards.

Key responsibilities

- Coordinate Test Cycles - plan scope, estimate cycle length, invite testers and assign tasks.
- Create and update mindmaps, test cases, test suites and monitor the execution of those suites.
- Measure, control and write summary reports on the testing progress and results.
- Solve technical challenges such as obtaining logs or simulating geographical location.
- Support testers by answering their questions in their work and escalating any project blockers.
- Review testers work and give them meaningful feedback with suggestions for improvements, to nurture their development.
- Engage in client communication as per the guidance of the Project Manager.

What do we expect from you?

- Hands on experience in software testing and writing tests.
- Experience leading testing activities on large, complex projects.
- Exposure to software testing patterns, frameworks, and methodologies.
- Passion, curiosity and focused interest in software testing.
- Quality is the fabric of your being.
- A testers mindset - analytical, detail oriented and able to manage time well.
- You love working with people and you’re passionate about changing the world.
