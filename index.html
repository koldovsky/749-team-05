About Cloudy Group

The best customer experiences are built with Cloudy Group. We give organizations the tools to understand their customers and engage with them in a more personal way. Our products are easy to use and implement. Cloudy Group gives businesses the flexibility to move quickly, focus on innovation, and scale as their business grows.

Benefits of working here

Health and wellness

Our health benefit is more than just a medical coverage for you and your family. You can go in for yoga or fitness classes and enjoy access to healthy produce, snacks, and a wellness discount program.

Learning and development

Full-time employees are reimbursed with our education program when they attend a professional conference or take courses to develop new skills within their career.

Rest and relaxation

We understand that you have life outside of work. That means plenty of PTO days for full-time employees and comfy couches to change a scenery or get some rest from your desk.

Planning for the future

No matter what the occasion, our benefits package keeps you covered — with a 401K match, 16 weeks of maternity/paternity leave, disability, and life insurance.

Our Values

Passion

We are passionate about everything we do – about our products, our company, and our industry. It is not just about the job: we share a passion to bring fun to everyone in the world and be the best in doing so.

Unity in Diversity

We emphasize diversity in all facets of the company, from whom we recruit to how we build teams and studios. It is by combining the diverse talents and backgrounds of our multicultural group that we achieve greatness.

We boldly embrace change and have the means and the will to tackle any endeavor. We are proud of our ability to adapt quickly and evolve, always staying true to our entrepreneurial spirit.

Employees Care

We care of material and moral side of each employee. Every person is an active and a key player in a bank working system. We care of our members to feel themselves at ease and being supported.

Autonomy

Flagging issues is not enough. We constantly push ourselves to act as leaders: to take initiative, challenge the status quo, and bring solutions to improve Cloudy Group every day. We do not shy away from adversity.

Talent Growth

We take chances on people. We favor taking a risk on promising aspirants who fit in our culture and see themselves staying with us over a long period.
